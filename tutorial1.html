Get Client ID and Client Secret

In this tutorial, you will learn how to get the Client ID and Client Secret for your application.

  1. Go to your provider's API management console.
  2. Navigate to the "Credentials" section.
  3. Click "Create New Credentials" and choose "OAuth 2.0 Client ID".
  4. Fill in the necessary details, such as application name and redirect URI.
  5. Click "Create" to generate your Client ID and Client Secret.
